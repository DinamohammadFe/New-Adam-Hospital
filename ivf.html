What is IVF?

In Vitro Fertilization (IVF) is an advanced fertility treatment where eggs are retrieved from the ovaries and fertilized with sperm in our specialized laboratory. The resulting embryos are carefully monitored and developed before being transferred to the uterus, offering hope to couples facing various fertility challenges.

IVF Treatment Step by Step

Your IVF journey is carefully planned and executed with precision. Here's what the process typically involves, so you can feel confident about your fertility treatment.

Initial Consultation

Meet with our fertility specialist to discuss your medical history, previous treatments, and assess both partners. We'll create a personalized IVF treatment plan tailored to your specific needs.

Ovarian Stimulation

You'll take hormone medications for 8-14 days to stimulate your ovaries to produce multiple eggs. Regular monitoring with blood tests and ultrasounds ensures optimal response and timing.

Egg Retrieval

A minimally invasive procedure performed under light sedation to collect mature eggs from your ovaries. The procedure takes about 20-30 minutes and you can go home the same day.

Sperm Collection & Preparation

On the same day as egg retrieval, sperm is collected and prepared in our laboratory. The healthiest and most motile sperm are selected for fertilization.

Fertilization

Eggs and sperm are combined in our specialized laboratory using conventional IVF or ICSI (Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection) techniques, depending on your specific needs.

Embryo Development

Fertilized embryos are carefully monitored and cultured in our incubators for 3-5 days. Our embryologists track their development and select the highest quality embryos for transfer.

Embryo Transfer

The selected embryo(s) are gently transferred into your uterus using a thin catheter. This simple procedure is usually painless and takes just a few minutes to complete.
