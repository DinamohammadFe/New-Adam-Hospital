What is IVF?

In Vitro Fertilization (IVF) is an advanced fertility treatment where eggs are retrieved from the ovaries and fertilized with sperm in our specialized laboratory. The resulting embryos are carefully monitored and developed before being transferred to the uterus, offering hope to couples facing various fertility challenges.
