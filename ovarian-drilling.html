What is Ovarian Drilling?

Ovarian drilling, also known as laparoscopic ovarian diathermy (LOD), is a minimally invasive surgical procedure used to treat women with polycystic ovary syndrome (PCOS) who have not responded to fertility medications like clomiphene citrate.

During the procedure, small holes are made in the ovaries using laser or electrocautery. This reduces the production of male hormones (androgens) and can restore normal ovulation patterns. The procedure is performed laparoscopically through small incisions, making it minimally invasive with faster recovery times.

Understanding PCOS and Ovulation Problems

What is PCOS?

  • Hormonal disorder affecting 5-10% of women
  • Irregular or absent ovulation
  • Elevated male hormone levels
  • Multiple small cysts on ovaries
  • Insulin resistance
  • Leading cause of female infertility

PCOS Symptoms

  • Irregular menstrual periods
  • Difficulty getting pregnant
  • Excessive hair growth
  • Weight gain or difficulty losing weight
  • Acne and oily skin
  • Hair thinning or male-pattern baldness

Who Needs Ovarian Drilling?

Clomiphene Resistance

Women with PCOS who don't ovulate with clomiphene citrate treatment.

Normal BMI

Particularly effective in women with normal body weight and PCOS.

High LH Levels

Women with elevated luteinizing hormone (LH) levels.

Avoiding Injections

Those who prefer to avoid daily fertility injections.

Reduce Multiple Pregnancy Risk

Lower risk of multiple pregnancies compared to fertility drugs.

Long-term Solution

Seeking a more permanent solution to ovulation problems.

Comprehensive evaluation including hormone tests and ultrasound.

Laparoscopic Surgery

Minimally invasive procedure through small abdominal incisions.

Drilling Process

Creating small holes in ovaries using laser or electrocautery.

Same-day Discharge

Outpatient procedure with quick recovery and minimal downtime.

How Ovarian Drilling Works

Reduces Androgen Production

Decreases male hormone levels that interfere with ovulation.

Restores Hormone Balance

Normalizes LH and FSH levels for regular ovulation.

Improves Ovulation

Restores natural ovulation cycles and fertility.

Considerations
