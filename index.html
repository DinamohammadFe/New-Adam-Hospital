I Want To Preserve My Fertility

Not the right time to start a family? At Adam International Hospital, we offer egg freezing, sperm freezing and embryo freezing for couples as a method of successfully preserving their fertility so that they can still start a family later in life. We also offer fertility preservation for those that are at risk of becoming prematurely infertile.
