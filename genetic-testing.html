What is Genetic Testing?

Preimplantation genetic testing (PGT) is a screening procedure used during IVF to identify genetic abnormalities in embryos before implantation. It includes PGT-A for chromosomal abnormalities, PGT-M for single-gene disorders, and PGT-SR for structural rearrangements. This helps select healthy embryos, reducing the risk of miscarriage, failed IVF, and inherited conditions.

When is Genetic Testing Recommended?

  • ✓ Women over 37 years old
  • ✓ History of recurrent miscarriages
  • ✓ Multiple failed IVF cycles
  • ✓ Family history of genetic disorders
  • ✓ Known carriers of genetic conditions
  • ✓ Prior child with chromosomal abnormalities
  • ✓ Sperm abnormalities
  • ✓ Fertility preservation for high-risk groups

Genetic Testing Step by Step

Initial Consultation & Assessment

Review medical history, family genetics, and discuss PGT options with a genetic counselor.

IVF Cycle Initiation

Ovarian stimulation, egg retrieval, and fertilization to create embryos.

Embryo Culture

Embryos are grown to the blastocyst stage (day 5-6) in the lab.

Embryo Biopsy

A few cells are safely removed from each embryo for genetic analysis.

Genetic Analysis

Samples are tested for chromosomal abnormalities or specific genetic conditions.

Embryo Freezing & Results

Embryos are frozen while awaiting results (1-2 weeks).

Embryo Selection & Transfer

Healthy embryos are selected and transferred to the uterus.

Advantages of Genetic Testing

  • ✓ Higher Success Rates

    Selecting chromosomally normal embryos improves IVF implantation and live birth rates.

  • ✓ Prevent Genetic Disorders

    Screens for inherited conditions like cystic fibrosis, reducing risk of affected children.

  • ✓ Reduced Miscarriage Risk

    Avoids transferring embryos with abnormalities that often lead to pregnancy loss.

  • ✓ Fewer IVF Cycles

    Increases efficiency, potentially reducing the number of treatments needed.

  • ✓ Personalized Selection

    Allows for tailored embryo choice based on specific genetic profiles and risks.

  • ✓ Cost-Effective Long-Term

    Prevents costly treatments for genetic conditions and multiple failed cycles.

  • ✓ Family Planning Options

    Enables gender selection for family balancing in some cases.

  • ✓ Emotional Reassurance

    Provides peace of mind knowing embryos are screened for major genetic issues.
